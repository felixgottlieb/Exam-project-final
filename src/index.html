01 VILDSKUD WEBSITE

『 Concept, Identity, Design, Development  』

During our Re-design course we were tasked with doing a radical redesign in collaboration with Vildskud Festival. The assignment was based around making a website with as small an impact as possible on the environment, the total size of the website allowed to be only 500kb.

In two weeks we redid the branding, researched, wireframed, prototyped, tested and developed a new design.

02 LEDERNE CORE QUADRANT

『 Art Direction, Digital Design, Prototyping  』

During my internship at Adapt I assisted the Design Lead working with a pilot project for Lederne creating an online tool for company leaders based on the core quadrant by Daniel Hofman. I helped with design research, ideating, digital design, animation and more.

03 IVALDE JEWELLERY
